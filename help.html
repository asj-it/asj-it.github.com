Help

We have a few help guides below to assist you in getting the most out of our app. We hope you enjoy the App. Any further queries - you can contact us from the settings tab in the App

How do I join a group?

To join a group you need to be invited to the group. When you are invited, you'll either get sent a link to click on or you'll get sent the group name and Group PIN

If you're sentr the a link, click on the link. From this link you'll be sent to our web page, where you can join the group and download the PedG app

If you are sent the Group's name and PIN

  • Open the app (Download the App) if you don't have it yet
  • Go to the Settings Tab
  • Click on Join Group
  • Enter the Group's name in PIN (not case sensetive)
  • Press Join

That's it. You've joined the group and can see how oher members of the group are getting on with their steps.
How Do I Create a group and invite my Friends?
  • Go to the Settings Tab, Press Create Group
  • Enter the Group's Name and Press Ok
  • Your Group will have been created and you'll be the first member
  • Details about your group will be shown.
  • To invite your Friends, go to your messaging App (Twitter, Facebook, iMedssage, Whats App etc.)
  • All details you need will already have been copied, all you need to do is paste them Here (long press brings up the Paste menu)
How Can People join my Group that I have already created?
  • Go to the Settings Tab
  • Under 'MY Groups' select the group you want people to join
  • Details about your group will be shown.
  • All details you need will already have been copied, all you need to do is paste them Here (long press brings up the Paste menu)
